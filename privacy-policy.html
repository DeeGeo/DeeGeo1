Privacy Policy

Blink Bright Flashlight App

Last Updated: June 15, 2023
App Version: 2.1.0

1. Introduction

Blink Bright Flashlight ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) about our users.

2.2 Anonymous Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app. This includes:

  • App version and installation information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Crash reports (without personal context)

3. App Permissions

Blink Bright requires certain permissions to function properly:

Camera

Required for flashlight functionality. We do not access or store any camera images or video.

Microphone (DJ Mode)

Used only for sound detection in DJ Blink mode. No audio is recorded or stored.

Bluetooth

Only for syncing with other devices in DJ mode. No device data is collected.

Notifications

For displaying flashlight status. Not used for data collection.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures for any collected data, though no system is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Please contact us if you believe we have inadvertently collected such information.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.

Important Notice

Extended use of flashlight features, especially in DJ Blink mode, may cause device overheating. We recommend:

  • Avoiding continuous use for more than 30 minutes
  • Monitoring device temperature
  • Taking breaks during prolonged use
